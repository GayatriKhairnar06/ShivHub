ShivHub is an all-in-one personal productivity and life management platform inspired by the power and discipline of Lord Shiva, designed to help users organize their tasks, documents, goals, and digital life in a structured and meaningful way. At its core is ShivSphere, the central Django-based system that interconnects various specialized modules: RudraPlanner for daily tasks and deadlines; VedaVault for storing notes and documents; Karmalog for logging activities and progress; Mahakal Reminder for timely alerts; Trishul Tracker for habit tracking; ChandraSync and ShaktiSync for data syncing; Kailasa Connect for collaboration; Lingam Locker for encrypted storage; Tapas Timer for focus with Pomodoro sessions.
